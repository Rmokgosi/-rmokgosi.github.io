About Boyki Livhu




‘’I’m not the best but I can be creative in capturing the moment’’.

My name is Boykie Livhu, photography is my passion and hobby.  I started my
photography using a mobile phone and as a guy that loves technology, I took it
to myself to explore and understand the fundamentals of photography and pushing myself to the limit with the limited resources I had.


When I started, I used to take pictures of flowers using my phone (Xiaomi Redmi note 5A prime) doing this gave me a motivation to take more pictures. Since I have started photography, I have shared my pictures across social media platforms inspire people with my photos. I believe that my hobby/passion will turn into something good in one of these good days.
